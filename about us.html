About Cassini

Cassini Technologies is an IT services and consulting company that offers the entire spectrum of technology and outsourcing solutions.

There is a huge gap in the service industry wherein the niche areas in technology are not covered efficiently by way of cost or by quality of delivery. Unjustifiably high quotations, dependency on freelancers and ever increasing consultation fees by service companies are concerns that disrupt project costs and timelines. We at Cassini understand that not every requirement can be highlighted at the start of the project and hence are very flexible with regards to changes that come up later in the project. We are dedicated to help organizations address their most vital business challenges in the most effective way possible.

We specialise in the areas of ERP (SAP, SAP VISTEX, Microsoft NAV and more), Digital Marketing, Application Development & a few more. Our talented team brings with them years of international experience providing innovative solutions to address the business challenges of today.

We know IT solutions inside out, IT is what we do.

At the heart of our success is our collaborative approach. We often get some complex and specific needs from our clients. It is our mission to go above and beyond our duties to deliver a solution that is fit for purpose and meets requirements while staying on-time and on-budget. For us, It's not just about completing a job, we believe in putting that extra effort in to ensure complete client satisfaction. The end result for clients - efficient and effective delivery, thoroughly researched and completely packaged.

Our project team understands that each step in the process is critical to the overall success of a projects implementation. There is very little we haven't seen, so we know what needs to be done and how we have to do it.

What helps our cause is that we are a fun loving bunch, but take our job seriously. We love what we do, and it shows in our work. This coming together of like minded people is what gives us an edge over our competition.
